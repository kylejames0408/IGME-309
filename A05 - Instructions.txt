Goal: Shoot the target circle until it explodes. 
      Then, a new target will be generated at a random location.
      Repeat shooting until you want to quit.

How to play:
- Press 'a' and 'd' keys to move the cannon horizontally. 
- Press 'k' and 'l' keys to rotate the cannon's launcher. 
- Press the spacebar to shoot bullets on the direction the launcher points towards.
