Usage: 
- Right click on the mouse and choose a color for the polygon you're currently creating.
    - This color will be used when starting a new polygon as well.
- Click on the screen with the left mouse button to add a vertex to the polygon. 
- Press 'Enter' key on the keyboard to finish the creation of the current polygon.
    - Single vertex polygons will be represented by a square point.
    - Two vertex polygons will be represneted by a line.
- Right click on the mouse and choose 'clear' to remove all polygons, including the one you're currently working on.
- Right click on the mouse and choose 'quit' to quit the program, or press the escape key.
